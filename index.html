The Ritual

How to Use

01

Warm the Oil

Place the bottle in warm water for 2 minutes. Warm oil penetrates the scalp far deeper than cold application, maximising botanical absorption.

02

Massage with Intent

Part hair into sections and apply oil directly to the scalp. Use fingertip pressure in circular motions for 5–10 minutes to stimulate blood flow and follicle activation.

03

Allow to Absorb

Leave for a minimum of 2 hours or overnight. Wrap hair in a warm towel to deepen penetration and allow the botanical actives to work through the night.

04

Cleanse and Reveal

Wash with a mild shampoo. With regular use — twice weekly — expect visible reduction in hair fall within 4 weeks and renewed shine within 2.

01

Prepare the Canvas

Splash your face with cool water. Cool water tightens pores and prepares the skin to receive the cleansing actives more effectively.

02

Lather Gently

Dispense a small amount and work into a lather between palms before applying to face. This activates the fermented rice water enzymes for maximum brightening effect.

03

Circular Cleanse

Massage into skin in upward circular motions for 60 seconds, paying attention to T-zone and pore-dense areas. The neem works as you massage.

04

Rinse and Glow

Rinse thoroughly with cool water. Pat dry — never rub. Morning and evening use delivers visible brightening, reduced blemishes and balanced skin in 3 weeks.

Our Story

Born in the Sacred Forest of Tirupati

Leevs was founded in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh — a city blessed by nature and divinity. Our founders, Leela Srinivas and Jeevitha, began with a single conviction: that the forest holds everything skin and hair need to flourish.

Growing up in Andhra Pradesh, surrounded by coconut groves, curry leaf plants and neem trees, they witnessed their grandmothers formulate remedies that outperformed any commercial product. Leevs is the refined expression of that inherited wisdom — brought to a modern standard without ever compromising on purity.

Every formula begins with the ingredient, not the market. We source, test and blend in small batches to ensure absolute potency. Our commitment is that every product bearing the Leevs name contains nothing your skin would need protection from.
